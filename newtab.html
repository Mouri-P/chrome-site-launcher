Edit Sites

Enable edit mode to rearrange, edit, or delete your sites.

When enabled, edit, delete, and advanced buttons will appear when hovering over site icons, even when edit mode is off.

In Edit Mode:

  • Drag sites to reorder them
  • Click edit button to modify a site
  • Click delete button to remove a site
  • Click "Done" when finished
